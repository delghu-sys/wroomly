# Wroomly Master Calendar — Product + Marketing (Jul 2026 → Aug 2027)

Built 2026-07-08 from primary research (UMich Registrar 2026–27 calendar, CGIS/
M-Compass study-abroad deadlines, Ross/Engineering incoming-exchange pages,
Michigan Housing move-in dates, industry internship-recruiting timelines) plus
the community intelligence in `docs/community-intel.md` and the marketing plan
(`docs/marketing-plan.md`). Every fixed date is cited in §6.

**The one idea:** Wroomly's growth is not steady — it's a set of **calendar-
triggered pulses**. Each target audience "activates" (finds out they're moving /
leaving, then starts looking) on a predictable schedule. Marketing = being in
the right channel the moment each audience activates, not before, not after. The
**Feb–Apr 2027 summer-sublet season is the year's Super Bowl** and the decisive
window for the 100+ confirmed-matches goal.

---

## 1. The four audiences and WHEN each one activates

"Activates" = the moment the housing need becomes real (offer/acceptance lands),
which is *earlier* than when they start searching in earnest. Marketing wants to
be present from activation through search.

### A. Incoming exchange / visiting students → **Wroomly DEMAND** (need Ann Arbor housing)
Verified finding: incoming exchange students **receive a `uniqname@umich.edu`
address as soon as they're admitted/nominated** — so they ARE eligible for
Wroomly's @umich.edu gate. This is a real, reachable target, and one almost
nobody else serves well (they arrive knowing nobody, renting sight-unseen from
abroad — exactly Wroomly's trust wedge).

| Their term | Home-school nomination + UMich app deadline | Admitted / @umich.edu issued | They search housing | Arrive |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Fall 2026** | ~Feb 1 2026 (past) | Spring 2026 | **Apr–Aug 2026** | Late Aug 2026 |
| **Winter 2027** | ~Sept–Oct 2026 | Late fall 2026 | **Oct–Dec 2026** | Early Jan 2027 |
| **Fall 2027** | **~Feb 1 2027** | **Spring 2027** | **Apr–Aug 2027** | Late Aug 2027 |

→ Two inbound search waves per year: **Apr–Aug** (fall arrivals, the big one) and
**Oct–Dec** (winter arrivals). These renters are sight-unseen and scam-anxious —
lead with verification + the "verify your landlord / never wire" guides.

### B. UMich students going abroad → **Wroomly SUPPLY** (must sublet their AA place)
The trigger is the study-abroad acceptance colliding with a 12-month lease they
already signed. CGIS/M-Compass deadlines fall near the **start of the term
before** departure; the LSA study-abroad scholarship window for fall/year-long
programs ran **Oct 1 – Mar 7**.

| They go abroad | They apply / get accepted | "I must sublet" realization | Sublet period to fill |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Winter 2027** (Jan–Apr) | Applied fall 2026 | **Oct–Dec 2026** | Jan–Apr 2027 → **winter sublet market** |
| **Fall 2027** (Sep–Dec) | Apply Oct 2026–Mar 2027 | **Spring 2027** | Fall 2027 → hard-to-fill "fall sublet" |
| **Winter 2028** | Apply fall 2027 | Oct–Dec 2027 | — |

→ Study-abroad supply concentrates in **Oct–Dec** (winter-abroad → list winter
sublets) and **spring** (fall-abroad, harder). These are prime "list before you
leave" outreach moments.

### C. UMich students with summer internships elsewhere → **Wroomly SUPPLY** (sublet AA place May–Aug)
The biggest, most reliable supply pulse. Offer timing by industry:

| Industry | Offers land | "I'm leaving for summer" realization |
|---|---|---|
| Finance / big tech / top consulting | **Dec 2026 – Feb 2027** | Dec–Feb |
| Mid-size, engineering, marketing, most others | **Feb – Apr 2027** | Feb–Apr |

→ Summer-sublet SUPPLY crystallizes **Jan–Apr 2027, peaking Feb–Apr** — the exact
window that makes Feb–Apr the Super Bowl.

### D. Summer arrivals into Ann Arbor → **Wroomly DEMAND** (need AA housing May–Aug)
Summer research (UROP), summer internships at UMich / Ann Arbor employers,
incoming grad students, med-campus summer rotations. They search **Feb–May 2027**
for a May–Aug stay — same window as the supply, which is what makes a marketplace
clear.

## 4. The two peak seasons (where to concentrate everything)

1. **Feb–Apr 2027 — Summer sublet season (THE decisive window).** Supply and
   demand both peak; it's where the 100+ matches are won. Everything from
   Nov–Jan is loading the spring. Miss this and the year's goal slips.
2. **Late Aug 2026 — Move-in / launch / scam peak.** Highest housing pain and
   attention of the fall; the launch moment and the identity-building
   scam-alert debut. Smaller than spring for *matches*, but the trust and
   awareness foundation the spring depends on.

Secondary: **Oct–Dec** (winter sublets + megathread + summer pre-loading) and
**Apr–Aug** (fall-exchange inbound demand — a quieter, high-trust audience worth
a dedicated, calm content track).

---

## 5. What to do with this calendar (operating rhythm)

- **Plan backward from the two peaks.** Content, rituals, and ambassador asks for
  a season are prepped one month ahead (Jan for Feb–Apr; Jul for Aug).
- **Match the channel to the activation.** Finance/tech interns activating in Dec–
  Jan → reach them where they are then, not in March. Fall exchange admits get
  their @umich.edu in spring → that's when the verification message lands.
- **The exchange-student track is separate and calmer.** These renters are abroad,
  scam-anxious, and sight-unseen — a dedicated FAQ ("renting from abroad safely"),
  the verify-your-landlord guide, and Match alerts serve them better than the
  campus-pulse noise. Low competition; high trust payoff.
- **Weekly 10-minute metrics** (per community-intel §metrics): listings by source,
  Match signups by source, megathread status, scam-alert engagement, unprompted
  mentions.
